Notice of Privacy Practices
Effective January 1, 2025 · HIPAA §164.520

CareGlow is committed to protecting your health information. This notice describes how your Protected Health Information (PHI) may be used and disclosed, and how you can get access to it. Please review it carefully.

Your Rights
  • Access your PHI — you may request a copy of your health records at any time.
  • Amend your records — you may request corrections to inaccurate or incomplete information.
  • Restrict uses — you may request limits on how your PHI is used or shared.
  • Confidential communications — you may request we contact you only in certain ways.
  • Accounting of disclosures — you may request a list of disclosures made of your PHI.
  • Request deletion — you may request deletion of your PHI subject to legal requirements.
How We Use Your Information
  • Treatment — to coordinate care with your providers and manage appointments.
  • Payment — to process insurance claims and calculate your estimated costs.
  • Healthcare operations — to improve app functionality and patient experience.
  • We do not sell your PHI to third parties.
  • We do not use your PHI for marketing without explicit authorization.
Data Security
  • Sessions automatically lock after 15 minutes of inactivity.
  • Your information is protected under HIPAA's Privacy and Security Rules.
  • You may contact our Privacy Officer at privacy@careglow.health with any concerns.

You must agree to all items above to use CareGlow. Your consent is logged with a timestamp.
