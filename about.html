About Me

I'm a multifaceted designer based in California. Though I began my career unsure of my direction, my path led me through Operations, where I developed a strong foundation in both data and visualization.

That curiosity for design and problem solving had always been there — and eventually it became impossible to ignore. That interest led to a deliberate career pivot into UX design and my introduction to the DesignLab UX Foundations program, where I built the formal craft to match the instincts I'd had all along.

Today, I strive to collaborate closely with clients to create modern, distinctive product experiences that merge analytical clarity with thoughtful design.
